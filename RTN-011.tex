\setDocAbstract{%
\es is defined as any science enabled through and including the first data release. 
This document provides the plans for an Early Science Program with the \vrolsst. 
It describes the strategy for obtaining observations during commissioning and in the early phases of the survey.
It outlines how the Rubin operations team will work with the science community to optimise the various aspects of the Early Science Program 
driven by opportunities for high-impact early science results.
It is a living document that will evolve over the course of the remainder of the construction project and up to \drone. 
}
